Media & Press Coverage

The following coverage relates to the first-ever VR Classroom Field Experiment in Pakistan, conducted at Information Technology University through the Virtuality Labs.

National TV Research aired live on ARY News covering the VR Classroom initiative at ITU Lahore.
National TV Research aired live on Samaa TV covering immersive learning with VR at ITU Lahore.
Print Featured in Daily Times — "ITU Lahore Launches First Virtual Reality Classes".
Online Featured in Academia Magazine — "ITU Launches VR Classes for Immersive Learning".
Documentary Covered in the official ITU YouTube documentary — "VR Classes at ITU | 1st time in Pakistan".
Government Project demonstrated live to the Chief Minister of Punjab during an official visit to ITU.
