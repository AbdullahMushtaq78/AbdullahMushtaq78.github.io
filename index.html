About me

Hi! I am a Computer Science Undergrad student at Information Technology University. I am working as a Research Intern at Intelligent Machines Lab (IML) under the supervision of Dr Mohsen Ali and Dr Rehan Hafiz. At IML, my main research focus is 3D Computer Vision, Novel View Synthesis, and creating a realistic dynamic synthetic dataset for real-time 3D reconstruction in collaboration with ETRI South Korea. While working at IML, I am also working on an NLP-based Psychotherapy chatbot at the Center of Speech and Language Technologies (CSaLT) in collaboration with Prof. Abbas Edalat from Imperial College London. Furthermore, I am working as a Teaching Assistant and Senior Lab Lead at Virtuality Labs at Information Technology University with Dr. Syed Ibrahim Ghaznavi. I have also worked as a Python Instructor at Seed Programming.
